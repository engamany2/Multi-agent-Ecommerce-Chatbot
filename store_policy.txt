1. EXCHANGE AND RETURN POLICY
------------------------------------------------------------
 
GENERAL RULES:
- Customers are entitled to a full refund or exchange within 14 days of delivery if the product is in its original, sealed packaging.
- If the product is defective or does not match the description on our website, the return period is extended to 30 days.
- For "Change of Mind" returns, the customer bears the shipping fees. The item must be unopened and unused.
- Refunds are processed via the original payment method within 7-10 business days after the item reaches our warehouse.
 
CATEGORY-SPECIFIC RULES:
 
Phones (iPhone 15 Pro, Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra, Google Pixel 8):
- Phones with activated software or accounts (Apple ID, Google Account, Samsung Account) cannot be returned unless there is a confirmed hardware manufacturing defect.
- Screen damage or physical damage discovered after delivery is not covered under the return policy.
- Factory reset must be performed before returning any phone.
 
Headphones & Audio (Sony WH-1000XM5, Apple AirPods Pro 2, JBL Flip 6):
- Headphones and earbuds can be returned within 14 days if unused and in original packaging.
- If ear tips or accessories are missing from the original package, the return will not be accepted.
- JBL Flip 6 speaker returns are accepted only if the product shows no signs of water exposure or physical damage.
 
Smartwatches (Apple Watch Series 9, Samsung Galaxy Watch 6):
- Smartwatches linked to an Apple ID or Samsung account cannot be returned unless there is a hardware defect.
- Watch bands and accessories can be exchanged within 7 days if unused.
 
Accessories (Anker 737 Power Bank, Logitech MX Master 3S):
- Accessories can be returned within 14 days in original packaging.
- Power banks showing signs of overheating, swelling, or misuse will not be accepted for return.
- Mice and keyboards with physical damage or missing parts are not eligible for return.

6. FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQs)
------------------------------------------------------------
 
Q: Can I open the package while the courier is waiting?
A: Yes, you can inspect the external condition of the box. However, internal testing of electronics is only allowed after payment for security and insurance reasons.
 
Q: Can I exchange my iPhone 15 Pro for a Samsung Galaxy S24 Ultra?
A: Yes, exchanges between products are allowed within 14 days, provided the product is in its original sealed condition. Price differences will be charged or refunded accordingly.
 
Q: What happens if my Sony WH-1000XM5 stops working after 6 months?
A: Your product is covered under the 1-year Sony warranty. Contact our support team and we will direct you to the nearest Sony authorized service center.
 
Q: My Anker power bank arrived damaged. What should I do?
A: Contact us within 48 hours of delivery with photos of the damage. We will arrange a free replacement or full refund.
 
Q: Can I buy the Apple AirPods Pro 2 on installments?
A: Yes! You can use ValU, Aman, or credit card installments. Contact us on WhatsApp for the latest available plans.
 
Q: How do I track my order?
A: Once your order is shipped, you will receive an SMS and email with a tracking link. You can also contact our support team with your order number for a status update.
 
Q: Is the Google Pixel 8 covered under warranty in Egypt?
A: Yes, we provide a 1-year local agent warranty for all Google Pixel devices purchased from Tech-Nova.
 
Q: Can I return the Logitech MX Master 3S if I don't like it?
A: Yes, within 14 days of delivery, as long as the product is unused and in its original sealed packaging.

Q: Can I return my iPhone if I don't like it after setting it up?
A: No. Once an iPhone or any smartphone has been activated with an account 
(Apple ID, Google Account, Samsung Account), it cannot be returned unless 
there is a confirmed hardware manufacturing defect.
